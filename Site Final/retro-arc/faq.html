Frequently Asked Questions

What types of retro games do you offer?

We offer a big variety of retro games from the classic era, in which could be played on consoles such as NES, SNES, Sega Genesis, and more.

How can I purchase a game?

You can purchase games directly through our website by adding them to your cart, once you've completed a purchase, head back to the home page and you'll see the game you've purchased labeled "Play!" on the game card.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer refunds within 30 days of purchase if the game has not been played for more than 3 hours.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the contact form on our Contact Us page.
